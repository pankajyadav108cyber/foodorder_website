Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

Ordering

How do I place an order?
Placing an order is simple! Just browse our partner restaurants, select the delicious items you want, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your delivery address and choose a payment method to complete the order.
Can I cancel or change my order?
You can cancel your order for a full refund within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, cancellations are at the restaurant's discretion as they may have already started preparing your food. To modify an order, it's best to contact our customer support immediately.
Can I add special instructions to my order?
Absolutely! For each item you add to your cart, there's an option to add special instructions. You can specify your preferences for spice level, mention allergies, or any other request for the chef.

Delivery

How can I track my order?
Once the restaurant accepts your order, you can track it in real-time! Go to the "My Orders" section of your account to see the status, from preparation to the delivery rider's location on a map.
What should I do if my order is late?
We're sorry for the delay! You can check the real-time status in the app or website. Sometimes, delays are caused by high restaurant traffic or road conditions. If it's significantly late, please contact our support team, and we'll assist you.

Payment & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept a wide range of payment options including credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and popular mobile wallets. Cash on Delivery (COD) is also available for most orders.
How are refunds processed?
Refunds are typically processed back to your original payment method. The time it takes to reflect in your account can be between 5-7 business days, depending on your bank's policies.
