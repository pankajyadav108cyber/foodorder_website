Privacy Policy

Your Trust Is Important To Us.

Last Updated:

Welcome to Tandoor-e-Yadav. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. This policy applies to all information collected through our website, mobile application, and any related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register an account, place an order, or contact us. The information we collect may include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Order Information: Details about the food you order, the restaurant you order from, order time, and special instructions.
  • Payment Information: We use secure third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, Razorpay). We do not store your full credit card or bank account details on our servers.
  • Usage and Device Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and browsing activity on our Services.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we may collect your precise location to help you find nearby restaurants and to facilitate deliveries.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders, including sharing necessary details with restaurants and delivery partners.
  • Create and manage your account.
  • Communicate with you about your orders, promotions, and updates.
  • Personalize your experience and recommend restaurants or dishes.
  • Improve our Services, customer support, and business operations.
  • Prevent fraudulent transactions and enhance the security of our platform.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with the following third parties:

  • Restaurants: To enable them to prepare and fulfill your order.
  • Delivery Partners: To allow them to deliver your order to your address.
  • Service Providers: Including payment processors, cloud hosting services, and marketing partners who help us operate our business.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, our users, or others.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure, so we cannot promise or guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access, update, or correct your personal information through your account settings.
  • The right to opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us.
  • The right to request the deletion of your account and personal data.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by a "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy frequently to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
