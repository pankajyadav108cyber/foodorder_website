Refund Policy

Our Commitment To a Fair Refund Process.

Last Updated:

At Tandoor-e-Yadav, we strive to provide a delightful food ordering experience. However, we understand that sometimes things may not go as planned. This policy outlines the situations where a refund may be granted and the process for requesting one.

1. Eligibility for a Refund

You may be eligible for a full or partial refund in the following situations. All refund requests are investigated and are subject to the sole discretion of Tandoor-e-Yadav.

  • Wrong Order Delivered: If you received a completely different order than what you placed.
  • Missing Items: If one or more items from your order are missing.
  • Significant Delays: If your order delivery is delayed by an unreasonable amount of time from the estimated delivery time.
  • Food Quality Issues: If the food is spoiled, contaminated, or clearly not fit for consumption. We may require photographic evidence.
  • Order Cancellation: If we or the restaurant partner have to cancel your order for any reason after your payment has been processed.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

We are generally unable to provide a refund in the following cases:

  • Change of Mind: If you cancel the order after the restaurant has already started preparing it.
  • Incorrect Delivery Address: If you provided a wrong or incomplete delivery address, resulting in a failed delivery.
  • Personal Taste: We cannot issue refunds for food that you simply did not like the taste of.
  • Minor Issues: Minor spills or slight variations in presentation that do not affect the edibility of the food.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you must contact our customer support team within 2 hours of receiving your order. Please follow these steps:

  • Navigate to the "Help" or "Support" section in our app or website.
  • Select the relevant order from your order history.
  • Clearly state the issue and provide your order number.
  • If the issue is related to wrong items or food quality, please attach clear photos as evidence. This helps us resolve your case faster.

4. Refund Process

Once we receive your request, our team will review the case. We will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment or to your Tandoor-e-Yadav wallet within 5-7 business days.

5. Changes to this Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. The updated version will be indicated by a "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
